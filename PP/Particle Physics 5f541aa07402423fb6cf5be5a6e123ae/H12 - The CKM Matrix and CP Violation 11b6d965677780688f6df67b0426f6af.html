CP Violation

  • Modern day universe is matter dominated with matter/anti-matter asymmetry at
    • One possible explanation is that for every 10910^9109﻿ anti-baryons in the early universe there were 109+110^9+1109+1﻿ baryons, which annihilated to 111﻿ baryon +∼109+\sim 10^9+∼109﻿ photons and no anti-baryons. For such an asymmetry from a symmetric initial state, we require:
      • Baryon number violation
