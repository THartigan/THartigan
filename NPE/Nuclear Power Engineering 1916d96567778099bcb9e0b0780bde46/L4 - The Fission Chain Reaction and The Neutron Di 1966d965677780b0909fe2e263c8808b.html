  • The power distribution can be improved (decreasing the form factor) by surrounding the core with a reflector material with high neutron scattering cross-section, hence reflecting leaking neutrons back into the core.
    • Good moderators tend to be good reflectors.
    • Note, this reflection improves neutron economy, so reduces the critical mass.
