Motivations for Curvature in Spacetime

Mathematical

  • SR introduces the link between time and space - by Lorentz boosting, an interval between events in time for one observer can be made to appear as an interval in space for another.
    • Therefore, as typical examples of curvature are surfaces embedded in 3D space, it would be weird for curvature to be applied only to space and not to time as well.
